Meatloaf

This meatloaf is a family favorite

Ground beef combined with aromatic spices, onion, garlic, and more to create a juicy and savory dish that everybody will love!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Tomato
  • Garlic
  • Bread crumbs

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl add the meat, tomato sauce and bread crumbs
  2. Finelly chopp garlic and onions and add to your meat
  3. Mix in spices and mix everything togheter until well incorporated
  4. Bake in the oven at 350F for 35 minutes
