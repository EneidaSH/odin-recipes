Tomato Soup

One spoonful of tomato soup has the power to automatically transport you back to your childhood.

Ingredients

  • Tomatos
  • Chicken broth
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Boil the tomatoes, onion, garlic, and broth.
  2. Run the mixture through a food mill into a large bowl
  3. Add the tomato mixture and season.
  4. Bake in the oven at 350F for 50 minutes
